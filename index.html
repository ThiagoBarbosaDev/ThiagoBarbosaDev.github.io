About Me

Hello World! My name is Thiago Barbosa, a brazilian web developer hopeful. I hope that my journey into web development will provide me a way to reconcile a living with something I live for, which for me, is to create.

When creating, I channel my creativity through Photoshop (which I use extensively for doing digital drawing/painting and the ocasional meme) and I can do simple tasks with the rest of the adobe suite (simple SVGs on illustrator, simple video editing on premiere pro).

Currently I am studying Web Development, hoping to be a proficient full-stack developer, but my aspiration is to work with front-end and perhaps expand into design, UI and UX. Recently I have created a Github and a LinkedIn.
